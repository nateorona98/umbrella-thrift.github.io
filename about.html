About Umbrella Thrift

Welcome to Umbrella Thrift! We are located in downtown Hobart, Indiana. We carry gently used clothing and accessories for all ages and genders. Come visit us at our lakefront store, grab a bite to eat, and pick out some great pieces for your wardrobe at great prices!

Reducing our carbon footprint

At Umbrella Thrift, we believe in reducing our carbon footprints by shopping second hand. The fast fashion industry is one of the leading contributers to pollution, and our goal is to provide the highest quality options to our community for the lowest costs and least amount of stress on our environment. Shop local, shop secondhand, save the world!

Get Discounts For Donating

Another of our goals at Umbrella Thrift is to help those in need. There is always a free bin of clothing outside the store for those in need, and that is driven by donations from you. If you bring in a bag of clothing to donate when you shop, you will get a 20% discount off any purchase with us. Come by and shop or donate!

The Owner

Hi, I'm Amanda! I live locally here in downtown Hobart with my husband, two kids and million animals. I'm super excited to serve the community by running a local thrift shop! As long as I've lived here, I've said that Hobart needs a good thrift shop, so I decided to open one myself in June 2020!
